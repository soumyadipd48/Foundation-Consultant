    About Us

    Incorporated in October, 1978 as a specialist firm, Foundation Consultants have able to grow its reputation for various projects in different parts of the country. Over the years it has served to its clients with quality professional services in the field of geotechnical investigations. Since then, Foundation Consultants has been able satisfy its clients with complete proper services. Since 1978 we have completed geotechnical investigations for more than 1695 multidisciplinary projects of various dimensions in different parts of our country. From the enclosed list of some of the projects for which we were entrusted with geotechnical investigation, it may kindly be recorded that the areas of our operation spanned from remote places in Arunachal Pradesh to Nagapattinam at extreme South in the state of Tamil Nadu. We take up detailed survey at various project sites apart from Geotechnical Investigations.
